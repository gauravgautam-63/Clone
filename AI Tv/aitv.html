Samsung AI TV innovations

8K AI Upscaling Pro

See lower resolution games, shows and movies beautifully upscaled into 8K. Hundreds of built-in neural networks improve your picture quality on the fly to produce our sharpest 8K picture yet.

Picture Quality

Revisit your favorites in a new resolution

Samsung Neo QLED 8K breathes new life into classic shows, movies and games with Al Upscaling. Embedded Al neural networks enhance scenes as you watch, transforming lower resolution content into hyper-clear 8K resolution.

Sound

Feel every scene

Get ready to lose yourself in the action. New top channel speakers blanket you in Dolby Atmos surround sound, delivering multi-dimensional audio you can experience as much as you hear.

Focus on the voices that tell the story

Active Voice Amplifier Pro isolates dialogue from background sounds both within the scene and within your room, ensuring you catch more lines with enhanced clarity.

TV and soundbar orchestrated in perfect harmony

Pair your Samsung Neo QLED TV with a compatible Samsung Soundbar to unlock dynamic Q-Symphony audio that optimizes every speaker between your devices, filling the room with next-level surround sound.

Smart TV

Do the SmartThings!

Our Samsung Neo QLED TVs come with an integrated SmartThings hub, giving you a one-stop shop to access and organize compatible smart devices around the house.
